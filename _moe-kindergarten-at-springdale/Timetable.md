MOE Kindergarten is a 4-hour Kindergarten 1 and 2 programme for children aged 5 and 6 years old.  
  
Due to the safe management measure, the K1 & K2 sessions are staggered. The timings are as follows:

AM Session

K1: 8am – 11.45am

K2: 8.15am – 12pm

PM Session

K1: 1pm – 4.45pm

K2: 1.15pm – 5pm
